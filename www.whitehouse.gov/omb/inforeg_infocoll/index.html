      • Small Business Paperwork Relief Task Force Report (June 28, 2004) (38 pages, 246 kb) This is the Final Report of the Small Business Paperwork Relief Task Force created under the Small Business Paperwork Relief Act of 2002 (SBPRA). In an effort to reduce paperwork burden on small businesses, the Federal government established an interagency Task Force to study and recommend additional means of reducing the burden on all small businesses. It contains findings and recommendations intended to reduce the burden imposed on small businesses by government paperwork information collection requirements. Also linked below is a list of compliance assistance resources and agency points of contact available to assist small businesses.

      The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) issued the Information Collection Budget of the United States Government (ICB) in June 2006. The ICB is OMB’s annual report to Congress under the Paperwork Reduction Act. On October 13, 2006, OMB received on a letter from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services of the Department of Health and Human Services providing a revised estimate of its annual paperwork burden. In a letter dated December 15, 2006, OIRA informed the House Committee on Government Reform that OIRA would post on OMB's website a revised version of the FY 2006 ICB on OMB's Web site that incorporates the revised burden estimate.  This is the revised version. For the original version issued in June 2006, see link below.
